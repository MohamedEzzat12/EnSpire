Mission & Vision

EnSpire is a youth non-profit organisation that was founded to spread business literacy knowledge to youth all over the world who do not have access to guidance or mentorship to begin their entrepreneurial journey and, more importantly, to find a community of like-minded peers to help them sustain their passion. We, as founders, aim to do this by leveraging the knowledge and experience we got from elite entrepreneurship and business programs. We believe this is our responsibility towards our community and the world.
